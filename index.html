Seven Lessons Learned

Next

One:
Create an Immersive Experience

  • Stick with your theme throughout the entire game.
  • Make your game flow from beginning to end.
Next

Two:
Experiment with Ideas

Next

Three:
Take Something Out of Your Game

  • Simplify your game - no really, find at least one or two things and take them out of your game
Next

Four:
Make Your Game Easy to Learn

  • Use the game's mechanics to educate the player.
  • Try not to rely on text to relay how to play the game.
  • Only introduce one mechanic at a time.
Next

Five:
Have Difficulty Increase Progressively

  • dont start out really hard, make it progressively harder as you go through the game (introduce new mechanics, more complex level design, etc...)
Next

Six:
Get People to Playtest Your Game

  • Get fresh perspectives on your game
  • This should not be overlooked
Next

Seven:
Make It Fun to Play

  • if your game isn't fun, no one will want to play it
