7 lessons learned in my vast experience producing video games

Next
Plan your vibe/concept - stick with a theme throughout the entire game, makes it cohesive Next
Experiment and push ideas Next
Simplify your game - no really, find at least one or two things and take them out of your game Next
Easy to learn - try not to use text to educate players, make the game a learning experience Next
Progressive difficulty - dont start out really hard, make it progressively harder as you go through the game (introduce new mechanics, more complex level design, etc...) Next
Get playtesters, very important Next
Make it fun - if your game isn't fun, no one will want to play it Next
